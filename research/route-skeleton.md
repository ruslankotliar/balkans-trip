# Draft day-by-day skeleton (v4 - 2026-06-12, conservative seed baseline)

Loop logic: Zadar -> Dubrovnik fast-ish (airport pickup Jun 18 evening), then a Dubrovnik-anchored loop: Mljet -> Bosnia inland -> Montenegro -> coast -> Dubrovnik. This is the conservative starting point; harder / richer variants still live in the research notes.

**Key changes from v1:** Biokovo/Sveti Jure are no longer baked into the default; Day 6 is Sarajevo-only rather than Boračko; Tara rafting stays on the Sarajevo->Montenegro crossing day; Day 8 is the mixed-group Durmitor day (Prutaš by default, Veliki Međed only for experienced scramblers); the deep-south day is kept to a half-day with Ada Bojana as the payoff, not a full coast detour.

## Resolved open questions (v4)

- Plitvice: NO - wrong direction, +4h driving, no swimming.
- Neum corridor: NO for Day 3 - Pelješac Bridge is the cleaner default.
- Mljet: Overnight preferred. Prapratno is the staging camp; no car reservation possible, so the queue strategy matters.
- Sarajevo: Keep the full overnight; it is the cleanest break before the Montenegro drive.
- Tara rafting: Do it on the Sarajevo -> Šćepan Polje crossing day so the route does not double back later.
- Deep south: Half-day yes, full-day only if the group explicitly wants to pivot toward kitesurfing and beach time.
- Durmitor hike: Prutaš is the default. Veliki Međed is a manual swap for the scramblers only.
- Kotor: Jun 27 remains the quiet-cruise target day.
