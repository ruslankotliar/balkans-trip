## 2. Recommendation

**Take Scenario A.** It is the only plan that implements every digest verdict (no Plitvice, Mljet-overnight, Sarajevo-yes, deep-south-skip, Kotor-on-Jun-27) AND every confirmed group wish from CLAUDE.md — including the joining member's own Montenegro route — while keeping the lowest risk profile, the cheapest bed line, and genuine slack in every day (digest §1, §3, §5). B buys three more summits at the cost of Dubrovnik, both islands, Skadar, Budva and the safe final border night — too much unanimous-verdict material sacrificed for gains the group hasn't voted on (off-route #4 explicitly says present Prokletije, don't decide unilaterally); C trades the trip's #1 experience (Tara rafting) and half of Durmitor for a second island and a fourth ferry queue. If the vote drifts mountain-ward, the cheapest upgrade is grafting B's Day 8 (Trnovačko via 4x4 + Etno Selo Grab night) into A between Days 8–10 by giving up the Dubrovnik day — every other hybrid breaks the 5h cap or the Jun 27 Kotor pin.
