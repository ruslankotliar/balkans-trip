## Day 1 — confidence 68/100
The day now has a sane shape — one nature anchor (Krka), a food stop, a camp with an arrival swim — and survives dropping items, which fits the "easy first day" principle. The two real soft spots are timing and taste: with a 10:00 pickup you hit Krka at ~11:45, which every source calls the worst window (peak heat, peak crowds, €40pp), and Skradinski buk is a look-don't-touch no-swim boardwalk for a water-loving group on a hot day. It works as committed, but it is mid-day-crowd-exposed and the prime swim hours go to a waterfall you can only photograph.

- **NOTE** Krka National Park (Skradinski Buk) — arrival timing (med): With car pickup 10:00 (realistically rolling ~10:45 after paperwork + the cross-border-permit conversation) you reach Krka ~11:45 — the single worst window. Every source says arrive before 09:00 or after 15:00; midday in June = peak boardwalk crowds, peak heat, and the full ~€40pp peak ticket. You can't realistically beat the crowd on a 10:00-pickup day. Pre-book the timed entry online for ~12:00 (Lozovac entrance: big free parking + park shuttle bus down to the buk; Skradin entrance fills up and the boat queues badly in peak summer — pick Lozovac). Budget 2.5h and be rolling out by ~15:00 to keep the day from sliding. Verified June 2026 conditions: swim still banned at Skradinski buk since 2021; peak adult ticket ~€40.
- **NOTE** Krka swim expectation / Ramova as the real cooldown (med): Skradinski buk is a walk-and-photograph stop, not a swim — set that expectation now so the water-group isn't let down arriving hot at noon. Krka's three legal swim spots (Roski slap, Stinica, Pisak) are all far up-park and off your route — not worth chasing. The fix is already in the plan: Ramova Beach below Camping Krvavica is the arrival cooldown (quiet pebble cove, clear water, pine shade). Treat Krka as the morning culture/nature hit and Ramova as the swim payoff — that sequencing is correct, just make it explicit so nobody expects to swim at the falls.
- **NOTE** Kantun Paulina — Split-center detour, and it belongs on D1 (not D2) (low): The pin's own bestTime note ('Day 2, en route Paklenica->Omis') is stale — that Paklenica overnight no longer exists and D2 (Biokovo/Omis/Makarska) never goes near Split. Open daily 08:00-22:00 and cash only (carry euros). If you're tired/behind after Krka, this is the cleanest cut — grab food at the camp/Promajna instead and the day still works.
- **KEEP** Day shape / rest buffer (low): Demoting skydiving to optional and anchoring on a single big stop (Krka) gives this arrival day the right rhythm: active-ish morning, food midday, beach swim + camp setup before dark (sunset ~20:30 mid-June leaves slack).
