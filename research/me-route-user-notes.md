# Montenegro route — group member's notes (translated from Ukrainian)

Source: group member, received 2026-06-10. These are confirmed group wishes; pins live in `src/data/me-user-notes.json` with status `shortlist`.

## Route (if entering Montenegro from Bosnia via Šćepan Polje)

1. **Šćepan Polje border** → drive the **Piva river canyon** through **Plužine** → on to **Žabljak**.
2. **Žabljak / Durmitor NP**: hiking around **Black Lake (Crno jezero)** and a few peaks.
   - Planned hike: [Crno jezero → Veliki Međed (Sjeverni)](https://www.outdooractive.com/en/route/mountain-hike/opstina-zabljak/mountain-tour-from-crno-jezero-black-lake-to-veliki-meded-sjeverni/343480610/)
3. After Durmitor head to southern Montenegro. **Žabljak → Kotor**, stopping along the way:
   - **Ostrog Monastery** (mountain monastery)
   - **Skadar Lake** — one of: kayaking on the lake, OR going up to a winery, OR skipping ahead to the sea (**Sveti Stefan beach**)
   - **Budva**
   - **Kotor**
4. **Kotor**: short climb to the old fortress (San Giovanni), walk the old town.
5. After Kotor → back to **Dubrovnik**.

If we instead enter Montenegro directly from Croatia (coast side), it's the same route in reverse.

## Optional (only if Croatia leaves us time)

- **Komovi mountains** — summit one of the peaks, or a light hike. (East Montenegro, off the main route.)
