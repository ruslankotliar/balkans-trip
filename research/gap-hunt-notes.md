## Angles that came up empty (so nobody re-digs)

- **June 2026 festivals in the corridor:** thin. Mostar Summer Fest is **Jul 2–4, 2026** (misses the trip by days), Lake Fest at Krupac is August, Wine Fine Trebinje was May 2, Dubrovnik Summer Festival starts Jul 10. Nothing pin-worthy inside Jun 16–28 was verifiable.
- **Free thermal springs:** experiences-notes already nailed it — essentially none wild on route; no new evidence found.
- **Share Montenegro "underrated places" series (local forum-style):** everything relevant already pinned (Grebaje, Komovi) or consciously backup'd (Murići).
- **Vodopad Istup (Stolac):** surfaced in Croatian articles as a "hidden pearl, hard to reach" — could not verify coordinates or any first-person access report to the pin-quality bar. Left out on the wrong-pin-is-worse-than-no-pin rule.

## The 3 best finds, argued

1. **Pasjača Beach (`hr-pasjaca-beach`)** — The single clearest miss of the whole fleet. The trip is *anchored* to Konavle twice (airport pickup Jun 18, car drop-off Jun 28 side), the skeleton literally asks "sleeping that night towards... Konavle?", and the zone had zero pins — while containing Forbes' 2024 "most beautiful hidden beach in Europe", a cliff-stair drama swim that's exactly this group's taste. Converts two logistics days into highlight days for free.

2. **Žuta tabija (`ba-zuta-tabija`)** — Sarajevo is a live overnight option (+13 min driving per drive-times) and the data covers its food, clubs, tunnel and bobsled — but not the one thing every local does on a June evening: sunset on the Yellow Fortress with drinks. Free, 15 min from the pinned Baščaršija, and it's the "meditation on a mountain, party after" hybrid the group profile begs for.

3. **Kupari anti-trap + Fort Goražda swap (`hr-kupari-bay` → `me-fort-gorazda`)** — One find in two pins: the famous urbex the group would almost certainly google (Kupari) died under demolition in 2025, and the best replacement is a free abandoned Austro-Hungarian fort with the world's last 100-ton rotating turret, sitting on a road already in the plan (Kotor→Njeguši serpentines). This is exactly the "AI considers some bullshit" failure mode pre-empted: stale internet says Kupari is open; the 2025 ground truth says go to Goražda instead.
