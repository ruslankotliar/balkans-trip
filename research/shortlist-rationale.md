# Shortlist rationale (curation pass, 2026-06-10)

63 places promoted from `candidate` → `shortlist`, grouped by trip leg. Selection priority: (1) named must-do / top-ranked in a session's notes or `verdicts-digest.md`, (2) strong multi-source community evidence, (3) route fit, (4) variety per leg (sights + activity + swim + food + sleep). Nothing was demoted; everything else stays `candidate`. Also fixed: removed the duplicate `me-debeli-brijeg-border` entry from `me-places.json` (the canonical pin lives in `logistics-places.json`).
