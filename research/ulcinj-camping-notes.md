# Ulcinj Camping — Velika Plaža & Ada Bojana Research Notes

_Session: deep-research pass, June 2026_

## Summary verdict

Three viable camping options exist for the Ulcinj/Velika Plaža/Ada Bojana area, ranging from a brand-new well-facilitated site to the established nudist island camp. All are on or within 1km of Montenegro's best sandy beach. The deep-south extension is camping-friendly and budget-compatible.

---

## 1. Safari Beach Camping, Velika Plaža
**GPS:** 41.905°N, 19.271°E (confirmed via camping.info)
**Location:** Directly on Velika Plaža beach, ~5km from Ulcinj old town, ~4km from Ada Bojana bridge. In a pine forest above the sand — you camp where you swim.

### Pricing (4 adults + tent + car, June 2026)
- Low season (pre-July): **~€25/night all-in** — includes vehicle, tent/caravan, electricity, sanitary access, tourist tax. Confirmed by Balkan-turizam trip report (Serbian-language source, real traveler).
- Peak (July–Aug): ~€33/night per ACSI/camping.info listings.

### Facilities
- New showers with hot water (repeatedly praised in 2025 reviews)
- Cleaned "around the clock" per May 2025 camping.info reviewer
- Electricity hook-up, restaurant, beach bar
- WiFi reportedly unreliable (Aug 2025 reviewer)

### Community signal
- Strong on Serbian-language travel forums; described as "very clean, very relaxed atmosphere, welcoming community"
- camping.info Dec 2025: "Good site, open all year"
- One negative: voltage issues on some hook-ups (Aug 2025)
- **Rating: 4/5** — best-documented option, solid showers, right on the beach

## 2. FKK Nudist Auto Camp, Ada Bojana Island
**GPS:** 41.8647°N, 19.3416°E (park4night confirmed; eurocampings: 41.86472, 19.34447)
**Location:** On Ada Bojana island itself, 6km south of Ulcinj. Access via bridge/road. €5 entry fee to island, deducted from your camping cost.

### Pricing (4 adults + tent + car, June 2026)
From official site (visitadabojana.com):
- Tent: €5 | Car: €4 | Tourist tax: €1.20/person/day
- **June total: ~€25–26/night** (low-season rate May 1 – July 5)
- Peak (July 6 – Aug 24): tent €6, car €5 → ~€27–28/night

### Facilities
- 4 bathrooms, 16 toilets for ~220 max guests
- Showers: mass communal (no individual cabins). Park4night May 2026 review confirms **new sanitary facilities with hot water now installed** (renovation confirmed). Older reviews called them "a cesspit" — those are now outdated.
- 56 electricity hook-ups
- Supermarket, restaurant, tennis courts
- **CRITICAL: Water is NOT drinkable on-site. Bring your own.**
- Mosquitoes in the evening — bring repellent

### Nudist question
Multiple independent reviewers confirm: **nudism is not mandatory**. Non-nudist beach sections exist. The island is FKK-oriented in culture but clothed visitors are accepted. "We are not nudists and there are non-nudist beach [areas]" — camping.info reviewer.

### Stilt-house fish restaurants
Misko (since 1991), Ćirkova Čarda, Konoba Kod Ranka — all on the island's Bojana riverbank. These are the trip highlight for many non-campers too; worth a sunset dinner stop regardless of whether you sleep here.

### Kitesurfing note
The kite centres are at **Velika Plaža** (across the bridge), NOT on Ada Bojana island itself. Ada Bojana beach is calm — better for swimming and general beach time.

### Community signal
- park4night: 3.1/5 (30 reviews) — lowest rated but improving post-renovation
- camping.info: 3.5/5 (20 ratings) — facilities old but beach excellent
- **Rating: 3/5** — budget-cheapest option (~€25/night), and the island setting + fish restaurants are genuinely special, but the sanitary situation needs managing (bring water, go before mosquito hour)

## 3. Greenfield Campsite, Velika Plaža (NEW 2025)
**GPS:** ~41.905°N, 19.266°E (near road R-17, Donji Štoj — approximate from site description; 550m from Safari Beach per site text)
**Contact:** greenfield.campsite@gmail.com | WhatsApp +382 68 725965

### Pricing (4 adults + tent + car)
From official site:
- Tent: €3 | Car: €4 | Adults: 4 × €5 = €20
- **Total: €27/night** (plus optional tourist tax ~€4 = €31 all-in)

### Facilities
- New 2025 construction: "clean showers and toilets"
- Fresh water, wastewater disposal
- Electricity €5/day extra
- WiFi, BBQ, children's play area
- 550m walk to Velika Plaža via shaded trail

### Community signal
- No independent reviews yet (site opened 2025, limited data)
- Best-maintained facilities on paper; pricing transparent
- **Rating: 4/5** (on paper — no traveler confirmation yet, but no bad reports either)

## 4. MCM Camping (NOT recommended for this group)
**GPS:** 41.90059°N, 19.27545°E
**Location:** On Velika Plaža, ~7km from old town

### Why we're not recommending it
- camping.info 2.4/5 (recent 2025 reviews)
- Showers don't drain ("foot bath"), electrical supply runs at 160-190V (appliances don't work)
- Forced cancellation disputes and eviction reports (2025)
- Beach sunbeds €15-20/day extra — on top of camping fees
- Tripadvisor July 2025: "Too expensive for the price and nothing to offer"
- One positive cluster: June 2025 reviewer praised sanitary cleanliness and beach access — but overall pattern is negative

## 5. Miami Autocamp (backup / not researched in depth)
Located "just next to Velika Plaža, under pine trees, 4km from old town." Two toilets with showers (clean per 2019 report). One 2024 review gave 1-star: "muddy water, overcrowding, loud music." No 2025 data. Treat as backup if Safari Beach is full.

## Deep-south extension context

For the group: if you extend south past Budva to Ulcinj, you're adding ~2h extra driving vs turning at Kotor. The camping here is the cheapest in Montenegro (€24-31/night for 4) and Velika Plaža is dramatically better than anything further north. The stilt-house fish restaurants on Ada Bojana are a rare experience not replicated elsewhere on the route. June is ideal timing — before the July-August peak. Strong argument for the deep-south extension if even one night is available.

## Recommended combination
- **Night 1 in the area:** Safari Beach Camping (proven showers, on beach, best reviews, ~€25/night in June)
- **Day activity:** Drive to Ada Bojana for the beach + sunset dinner at Misko or Ćirkova Čarda
- **If you want to sleep on the island itself:** FKK Camp Ada Bojana (~€25–26/night, confirm new sanitary facilities, bring drinking water)
- **Greenfield** is worth a WhatsApp enquiry for current availability — newest facilities, honest pricing
